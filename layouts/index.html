Preface

In 1996, Andy Grove wrote Only the Paranoid Survive to describe the moment when a business faces a force so powerful that it changes the very foundations on which the business operates. He called it a Strategic Inflection Point. He had lived through one at Intel. He wrote the book so others might recognize the next one before it was too late.

This is a story about the next one.

Vikram is a fictional CTO. Arcline is a fictional company. But the inflection point they face is not fictional at all. Between 2022 and 2026, artificial intelligence went from a curiosity to a force that reshaped every knowledge-work industry on earth. I wrote this story to explore what that transition feels like from the inside — the denial, the delay, the loss, the reckoning, and the hard, unfinished work of transformation.

The characters and company are composites, drawn from real patterns I have observed across the industry. The emotions are real. The lessons are real. I wrote it for the executives, engineers, and knowledge workers who sense the ground shifting under their feet and are not sure what to do about it.

— Anshu Avinash, March 2026
